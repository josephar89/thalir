Who We Are

Rooted in Compassion, Dedicated to Your Welfare

The Thalir Capuchin Animation Centre is a dedicated welfare organization located in Arasur, Villupuram. Our name, Thalir, signifies a tender sprout—representing our commitment to nurturing the vulnerable and fostering new beginnings.

Guided by Franciscan values of peace and fraternity, our professional team provides a safe environment for individuals facing life's challenges. Beyond counseling, Thalir is deeply committed to the social upliftment of the poorest, including special services for Primitive Tribal people (Irulars), Transgender communities, Gypsies, the physically challenged, and the rural poor.

  • Holistic mental health and family counseling
  • Dedicated social outreach programs for marginalized communities
  • A continuously developing, peaceful campus in Arasur

A Capuchin Mission Initiative

Thalir Centre is a proud initiative of the Capuchin Franciscan Friars of the Amala Annai Province, Northern Tamil Nadu.

Our Expertise

Professional Care & Guidance

We offer specialized therapeutic services tailored to meet the psychological, emotional, and social needs of our community.

Family Conflict Counseling

Guided mediation and structured therapeutic support to resolve disputes, foster deep understanding, and restore harmony within the family unit.

Community Upliftment

Dedicated social services focused on empowering Irulars, transgender individuals, gypsies, and the rural poor towards sustainable livelihoods.

Psychological Assessments

Professional, strictly confidential evaluations to better understand individual psychological needs and tailor a highly effective therapeutic approach.

Venue & Facilities

Retreats & Spiritual Animation

Beyond our counseling services, Thalir Centre is open for all types of spiritual animation, including retreats, religious meetings, and seminars. We offer a deeply peaceful atmosphere surrounded by lush greenery, complete with comfortable accommodation facilities for our guests.

Highly Accessible: Conveniently located right on the Trichy - Chennai National Highways, just behind VRS Engineering College.

Online Program Highlights

Diverse Curriculums

Including six-month Franciscan Spirituality courses, six-week holistic spiritual growth programs, Leadership training, and proficiency courses in Italian, Spanish, and English.

Broad Audience

Serving Religious Leaders, Major Superiors, Formators, Teachers, Students, Research Scholars, and College professors. Facilitated by expert resource persons including Bishops, Jesuits, Salesians, and Religious Sisters.

Accessible to All

Programs were offered gratis to participants from Africa, South America, Pakistan, Sri Lanka, Bangladesh, Myanmar, as well as all Cloistered sisters and Capuchins.

"Funds raised through our global online initiatives have directly supported the social upliftment of the poorest in our community and vital infrastructure improvements at the Thalir campus, including new concrete roads, an RO System, a Computer Centre, campus fencing, and extensive environmental planting."
